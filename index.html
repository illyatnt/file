We can provide services such as: 

Daily/Weekly Care:

Check oil level.
Check fluid levels (brake fluid, coolant, windshield washer fluid).
Check tire pressure.
Clean and check the operation of headlights and taillights.
Clean front and rear windows.
Regular Maintenance:

Oil and filter change.
Check and replace air filter.
Check brake pads and discs.
Replace brake fluid.
Check exhaust system.
Check and replace drive belts.
Exterior and Interior Care:

Wash and wax the exterior.
Clean and polish windows.
Vacuum and clean the interior.
Care for carpets and seats.
Apply leather care products (if applicable).
Winter Care:

Install winter tires.
Use antifreeze in the cooling system.
Protect the body from salt and de-icing agents.
Preparing for Summer:

Replace winter tires with summer ones.
Check and replace coolant.
Check the air conditioning system.
Maintaining Electrical Components:

Check the battery and replace if necessary.
Check the ignition system.
Check and replace bulbs in headlights and taillights.
Anti-Corrosion Protection:

Regularly clean mud and salt from the underside of the car.
Apply anti-corrosion agents.
Engine Care:

Check the cooling system.
Check the condition of timing belts and replace them.
Check the operation of the fuel injection system.
